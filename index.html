About

Designed to be instantly inviting, then quietly deep.

doglikecat is an independent game company focused on casual puzzle, collection, and light strategy experiences. Our taste lives in the space between warm characters, crisp rules, and levels that reward a second try.

We prototype fast, polish slowly, and ship games that are easy to understand without feeling disposable.
