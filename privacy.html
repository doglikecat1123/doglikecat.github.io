Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how doglikecat collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our games, websites, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, support messages, playtest feedback, or other information you choose to send us.

When you use our games or website, we may automatically collect device and usage information, including device type, operating system, approximate region, game progress, crash reports, session length, interactions, and analytics events.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to operate our games and website, improve game balance and performance, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, measure marketing effectiveness, and understand how players experience our products.

3. Advertising, Analytics, and Third Parties

Our games or website may use analytics, advertising, attribution, hosting, payment, and platform partners. These partners may process information under their own privacy policies. We use these services to keep our games running, understand performance, and support optional ads or purchases where available.

4. In-App Purchases and Platform Services

If a game offers purchases, payments are processed by the relevant app store or payment provider. We do not receive your full payment card details. We may receive transaction status, product identifiers, and purchase history needed to provide purchased content and support.

5. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

6. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of your personal information. You may also be able to limit ad tracking or analytics through your device or platform settings.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we work to keep our systems responsibly maintained.

9. International Transfers

We may process information in countries other than where you live. When we do, we take steps intended to protect information according to this policy and applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and provide additional notice where appropriate.
